ABOUT US
VYK Network, a subsidiary of WE NETWORK LLC, specializes in the design and implementation of cutting-edge networks encompassing 2G through 5G technologies. We prioritize delivering maximum value to our clients. Successfully deploying advanced wireless networks requires deep expertise in planning, designing, and optimizing across various segments. Our core services revolve around comprehensive network solutions, serving both Telecom service providers and Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs). We are committed to providing a complete suite of services that elevate network performance and adapt to the ever-changing industry needs.

    Our Objectives

  • Conservation of natural resources and minimisation as well as utilization of waste by adoption of best practices.
  • Continual improvement of processes impacting Quality, environment and occupational health & safety aspects
  • Compliance with all statutory, regulatory and other requirements applicable to WE Network LLC.
  • Implementation of safe work practices and safety culture in all business operations.
  • Training and development of employees and contract workers and promoting their involvement.
